🧴 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All ingredient conflict checks are performed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No data is ever sent to a server.

2. Payments

Payments are processed securely by Creem, our third-party payment processor. When you make a payment, your payment information is handled directly by Creem and is subject to Creem's Privacy Policy. We do not store or have access to your full credit card or payment details.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage solely to remember whether you have unlocked custom ingredient features. This data never leaves your device. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to external services (e.g., Creem payment pages, Font Awesome CDN, Fuse.js CDN). These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

5. Children's Privacy

SkinClash is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@skinclash.com.

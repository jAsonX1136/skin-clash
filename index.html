FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about SkinClash and ingredient safety.

Is SkinClash really free?
Yes! All built-in ingredient checks are completely free, no sign-up required. You only pay if you want to unlock custom ingredient input — and even then, it's a one-time support contribution that unlocks access for weeks.
How accurate are the conflict rules?
Our conflict rules are based on published dermatological research and widely accepted skincare science. We focus on well-documented interactions between active ingredients. However, everyone's skin is different — always patch test and consult a dermatologist for personalized advice.
Is my data collected?
Never. All ingredient checks run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to a server, stored, or collected. SkinClash is 100% private by design.
Can I add ingredients not in the list?
Yes! Custom ingredient input is available when you support SkinClash. Once unlocked, you can type any ingredient name and test it against our built-in database. The unlock lasts 15, 30, or 60 days depending on your support level.
What payment methods do you accept?
We process payments securely through Creem, which accepts all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and various local payment methods depending on your country.
